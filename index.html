OUR PROJECTS
MODERN APARTMENT
The project involves the interior design of a two-bedroom apartment, with a focus on utilizing earth tones to create a warm and inviting atmosphere. The design aesthetic is rooted in modern elegance, combining sleek lines and contemporary elements to achieve a refined look.
PAMINTUAN CABANA
The project entails the creation of a compact pavilion, designed to function as a versatile hangout spot for the resident while also accommodating a small number of guests. The pavilion's design places a strong emphasis on accessibility and comfort, ensuring that it is a welcoming and inclusive space for all.
REDHOMES LOUNGE
This project involves the interior design of a living room within a UK residence. The design concept elegantly incorporates a striking navy blue and gold color palette, weaving these hues throughout the space to create a cohesive and visually appealing atmosphere.
SUMMIT RIDGE
This undertaking entails a comprehensive detailing of the interior fit-out design for a residential property located in the USA. The approach taken delves into intricate aspects of production, with a focus on ensuring the casework's realism and practicality to seamlessly transition into the construction phase.
HOUSE 1010
A residential duplex designed for modern living on a compact 100sqm plot of land. Each unit features two bedrooms and is meticulously crafted to maximize every inch of space while maintaining an elegant design language. With careful attention to detail, this duplex offers luxurious living in a beautifully efficient footprint, perfect for those seeking both style and functionality in their home.
HOUSE 28
This bungalow project places a strong emphasis on establishing a meaningful connection between the interior spaces and the diverse gardens that envelop and integrate with the house. The design ethos adopts a modern approach, while embracing the use of natural finishes to reinforce the design concept.

ABOUT FOLIARK
Foliark is an architecture firm based in Quezon City, Philippines. Our passion is to design spaces that enhance the human experience in the context of nature and the built environment. We believe that the intersection of art and science can create beautiful and functional structures that inspire and uplift people. Our team of architects and designers are committed to providing innovative solutions that meet the unique needs of each client. We take pride in our attention to detail and our ability to deliver projects on time and within budget.
OUR TEAM
At Foliark, we have a diverse team of architects and designers who share our vision of creating beautiful and functional structures. We work collaboratively to ensure that each project is a success. Our team members bring unique perspectives and skills to the table, allowing us to provide innovative solutions to our clients. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and delivering projects on time and within budget.
